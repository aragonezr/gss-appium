Location
Follow the red shuttle signs at the airport to the designated pick-up area. Shuttle buses are purple with white signage that reads Hyatt Regency O'Hare. International terminal call for pick-up.

Chicago McCormick Place Parking

Availability
24 hours, daily
Includes in/out privileges
Cost
Car - $14/day
Bus & RV's - $30/day
Location
Located at 31st St. near Lake Shore Drive. To get to the 31st St. lot from I-55, take southbound Lake Shore Drive and exit at 31st Street. Turn right and follow the parking signs. From downtown Chicago, take Lake Shore Drive South and exit at 31st St, turn right and follow the parking signs to Lot B.
